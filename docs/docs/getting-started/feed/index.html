Introduction The name CoinTAFT, TAFT itself is the pronunciation of the word Tough which means tough!

CoinTAFT is a secure Prof Of Work (POW) Blockchain technology platform that launch and announce on bitcointalk.org on 28 March 2023 with fair launch no pre-mine, and no ICO.

TAFT is a CPU mineable cryptocurrency and open source software developed under MIT license. It can be mined on GPU too, but CPU mining is more efficient. miners can choose a pool to mine at miningpoolstats.stream

TAFT will continue to push the limits of cryptography and blockchain technology through TAFT Project to support web 3.0  (Decentralized web, Semantic web,  Artificial intelligence, NFT, Smart Contract, Mobile Apps, Play to Earn).

What are the advantages of CoinTaft ?

Fast Transactions: CoinTAFT has a fast transaction time, which means that users can quickly send and receive transactions.

Privacy: CoinTAFT offers privacy features, such as stealth transactions and encrypted messaging, to protect user’s privacy.

Security: CoinTAFT is a secure blockchain platform  with 51% Attact Protection that uses advanced encryption algorithms to protect user’s funds and data.

Decentralization: CoinTAFT is a decentralized platform, which means that it is not controlled by any central authority. This ensures that the platform is not subject to censorship or manipulation.

Low Fees: CoinTAFT has low transaction fees, which makes it a cost-effective alternative to traditional payment methods.

Scalability: CoinTAFT is designed to be highly scalable, which means that it can handle a large number of transactions without slowing down.
